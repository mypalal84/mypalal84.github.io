Bobo

Don't you want to let curious Bobo into your home to take care of your baby? He will gladly be there as long as you keep feeding him bananas.

Pros:

  • Shows up on time.
  • Relatively clean.
  • Does not throw feces.
  • Potty trained (mostly).

Cons:

  • Has trouble differentiating between food and baby
  • Sheds
  • Banana dependent
  • Not actually a monkey, (see Chimpanzee)

Hannah

Hannah is probably more adorable than your kid, and will happily take them on field trips. Don't let her size fool you. Hannah is very good at using tools, which comes in handy while driving (for reaching the pedals).

Pros:

  • Looks like a baby
  • Babies love her
  • Affectionate
  • Clean driving record

Cons

  • Really really short
  • and fragile
  • most likely smaller than your child
